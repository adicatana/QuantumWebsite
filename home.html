Motivation

Nowadays, the edge of computing efficiency is represented by the exponential algorithms. The inovations discovered in physics are now combined with the theoretical work of quantum algorithmists. By making a leap from the classical 0 - 1 bit to the superstate that encapsulates both states at the same time, we can now solve exponential algorithms in linear time. If we can scale the current quantum computers such as D-Wave, we will finally be able to solve NP problems efficiently and make an impact on the whole world.
