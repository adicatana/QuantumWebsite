Geometric Interpretation of the Algebraic Operations
It is natural to let a complex number z = x + yi correspond to a point M(x, y) in the plane R x R. The point M is called the geometric image of the complex number z. The complex number z = x + yi is called the complex coordinate of the point M(x, y). We will use the notation M(z) to indicate that the complex coordinate of M is the complex number z.
