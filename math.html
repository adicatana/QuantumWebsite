Motivation

"God used beautiful mathematics in creating the world." - Paul Dirac
As in every scientific field, Mathematics is essential. Even though the mathematics of the classical and quantum systems is very different, similar phenomena show up in both places. The main difference is that in a classical system the hardware requiered is generally proportional to N whereas in the quantum system, the hardware is only proportional to log N.
The aim of this chapter is to provide the basics needed in further study and to show (once again) the beauty of Mathematics.
